Today's Python talk

Today, I gave a talk on Python – “An Introduction to the Python Programming Language” at Global Academy of Technology, Bangalore.
The audience (~60) were mostly the final year students of Info Sci. and Comp.Sci . Even if 5 out of 60 choose to do their final year projects using Python, I would consider my talk very successful.

Its always exciting to talk about Python, especially to those who are burdened with C/C++. I had to rush through the talk because I did not want to hold them back on a Saturday afternoon :).

I must be getting better at giving talks on Python. Its my third in 3 months :).
The next one (on wxPython GUI toolkit ) is on 21st May, in the BangPypers’ May Meetup.
